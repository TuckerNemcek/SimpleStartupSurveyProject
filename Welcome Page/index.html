Simple Financial Health Check

Welcome to the Simple Financial Health Check!

This survey is designed to gather some of the basic information from your Financial Statements to provide a limited but informative picture of the Financial Health of your business.

Please have your Income Statement (P&L) and Balance Sheet ready to fill this survey. For a more detailed monthly trend analysis and customized action items, please contact Simple Startup. We will email you the results of your Health Check so that you can keep it for your records. Enter your email and lets get started!
We'll never share your email with anyone else.
